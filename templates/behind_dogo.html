Behind DoGo 💙

DoGo (Donate & Go) is an intelligent donation management platform designed to connect donors with verified NGOs through a transparent, secure, and real-time tracking system. The project aims to simplify donation management while ensuring that donated resources reach the right people efficiently and responsibly.

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce wastage of usable food, clothes, and essential items by building a direct bridge between donors and verified NGOs. DoGo encourages responsible giving while ensuring transparency, accountability, and real-time donation tracking.

⚙️ How DoGo Works

1️⃣ Donor submits donation details
2️⃣ Donation request gets stored securely
3️⃣ NGOs access requests through dashboard
4️⃣ NGO accepts donation request
5️⃣ Status updates appear in tracking page
6️⃣ Donation gets completed successfully

🔐 Security & Verification

Only registered and verified NGOs are allowed to access donation requests. Each NGO provides verification details, NGO ID, contact information, and organization proof before accessing the dashboard. This creates a trusted and secure donation environment.

📦 Real-Time Tracking

DoGo provides a live tracking system where donors can monitor the current status of their donations. Status automatically updates from Pending → Accepted → Completed based on NGO dashboard actions.

🌍 Social Impact

DoGo helps reduce food waste, encourages community support, and ensures that essential resources reach people in need. The system promotes sustainable donation practices while increasing trust between donors and NGOs.
